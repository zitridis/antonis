Antonios Zitridis

Welcome to my webpage!

My name is Antonios Zitridis, and I am a 6th-year PhD student in mathematics at the University of Chicago. My advisor is Panagiotis E. Souganidis. I am originally from Greece and received a B.S. degree in mathematics from the National and Kapodistrian University of Athens.

Starting in Fall 2025, I will be a Byrne Assistant Professor in the Department of Mathematics at the University of Michigan, Ann Arbor, working within Professor Erhan Bayraktar's research group.

I am interested in Partial Differential Equations, Stochastic Analysis, and their applications. My PhD research is on interacting particle systems, McKean-Vlasov SDEs, stochastic optimal control, and mean field games.
